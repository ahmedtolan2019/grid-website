About Me

My name is Osama Fathi Zitoon and I'm a 23-year-old photographer and videographer located in Egypt. I study electrical engineering but photography has always been my passion. I love experimenting with all types of photography as you'll see in my gallery but astrophotography is the closest to my heart for the adventure it takes to be able to capture such photos in the depths of the desert. I want people to see the world through my lens as I see it, and especially to see Egypt and its unique unheard-of destinations. That's why I'm currently working on a project to tour Egypt and impress everyone with its beautiful spots. My goal is to inspire people to learn photography and start taking photos no matter the equipment or the lack thereof. I also want to inspire them to pursue their passion even if it's different from the track that life has placed them on. I just want to show you the beauty of the world like I see it through my photos and videos.
